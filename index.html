OBT Hungarian Picnic Grounds

About

13 acres of wooded area, with a creek that runs through it that is  conveniently accessible from #401 and # 407, close to the hamlet of Hampton, and the town of Bowmanville.

Events

Used for outdoor weddings, family reunion, various club events, church groups, the soccer field encouraged small tournaments, overnight camping, scouts.

Food

Kitchen contains two fridges, as well as a bar fridge, stoves, sinks, huge work area, serving window.

Amenities

Fully functioning women’s and men’s toilets with sinks.
Open soccer field, children’s playground area, small chapel area.
